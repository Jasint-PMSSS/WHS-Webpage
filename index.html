Welcome to Pentagon Parking

Parking at the Pentagon, Mark Center or Suffolk Building is managed by the Washington Headquarters Services (WHS) Parking Management Office and enforced by the Pentagon Force Protection Agency (PFPA). All vehicles must be registered with the parking office prior to parking at all three locations.

The Pentagon Parking Management Office:

Location: 2D1039, in the Pentagon Concourse adjacent to the Pentagon Federal Credit Union.

Hours of operation: 7:00 AM - 4:30 PM (M-F)

Pentagon Main Line: (703) 697-6251

The Mark Center Parking Management Office:

Location: Suite 03F21, of the West Tower.

Hours of operation: 7:30 AM - 4:00 PM (M-F)

Phone number: (571) 372-7100

 

If you need to request a Permit or a Clearance then please proceed to login. You must have a valid DoD CAC.

If you are having trouble logging in, then please contact Helpdesk at (xxx) xxx-xxxx

 

 

Disclaimer: “All visitors who have not met the PFPA requirements for pre-registration prior to arrival may experience lengthy wait times or may be denied entry to the building. Please follow this link for PFPA Pentagon Force Protection Agency (pfma.mil) for more information.”
